01 — About

Who I am

I'm an engineer currently working at NVIDIA, where I spend my days at the intersection of performance, GPUs, and low-level systems.

Outside of work, I build small projects for the fun of it — usually visual, usually algorithm-heavy, and almost always rendered in a grid of colored squares. Chess engines, pathfinding, fractals, a bit of computer vision on embedded hardware — whatever scratches the itch that week.

I'm open to interesting side projects — especially anything that mixes graphics, simulation, or performance-sensitive code. If you've got an idea and want a collaborator, I'd love to hear about it.
